I am back

Created2025-08-19|Updated2025-01-05

After a year, I have redone a personal blog website that I had previously created. This blog website is based on a previous project of mine, so there are not many changes in terms of technical selection and architectural design. However, over the past year, I have also learned many new technologies and tools, so in this remake project, I have added some new elements to make this blog website more complete and robust.

In the future, I will also consider adding some new features and optimizations to make the entire blog website’s user experience more fluid and friendly.

In short, this remake of the blog website not only allowed me to revisit my previous project, but also allowed me to learn many new technologies and tools, and integrate these new elements into the project to make this blog website more complete and robust.
